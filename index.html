American Psycho

American psycho is a horror/thriller movie from the year 2000 starring Christian Bale and directed by Mary Harron.
The movie is an adaptation of the book “American Psycho” written by Bret Easton Ellis.

The plot

The movie is about Patrick Bateman who is depicted as simply a psychopath who works on the New York Stock exchange.
He is a murderer and insane, trying to hide his real personality from his coworkers. As more time goes the more he loses
his sanity and starts going on his killing sprees.
